To-Do List

Instructions - Click to open/close
  • Entering Task
    • Enter the task in the field and press submit to create a new to-do item.
    • In the text field, press enter between tasks to separate them in to-do list.
  • Controlling the To-do item.
    • Press the Green button to mark the task as "Complete".
    • Press the Yellow button to mark the task as "On Hold"
    • Press the Red button to mark the task as "On Hold"
